About Blue Grid Media

Specialists in Google Local Services Ads

Focused on Local Leads That Convert
Blue Grid Media is a performance-focused digital marketing agency built to help local service businesses generate real, qualified leads through Google Local Services Ads (LSAs).

We don’t try to do everything. We specialize exclusively in setting up, optimizing, and managing Google Local Services Ads so local businesses get more calls, better lead quality, and a stronger return on ad spend.

Our team is made up of experienced marketers who have spent years working with local and professional service businesses through agency roles and hands-on freelance work. Collectively, we’ve worked with hundreds of Local Services Ads accounts across industries such as plumbing, HVAC, electrical, roofing, landscaping, cleaning services, and other home service businesses.

We’ve seen what actually impacts LSA performance — and what doesn’t. That experience allows us to avoid guesswork and focus on what drives rankings, lead quality, and cost efficiency.

Local Services Ads aren’t set-it-and-forget-it. Real results come from proper setup, continuous monitoring, and ongoing optimization. Our process is practical and data-driven, focusing on clean account structure, correct service categories and areas, smart budgeting, and regular lead quality reviews.
